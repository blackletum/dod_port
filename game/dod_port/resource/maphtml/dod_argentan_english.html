Mission Brief 20 August, 1944
US Army is making a final assault to liberate the town of Argentan. The Wehrmacht are waiting.

U.S. Army:
The "Station de Train" in the centre will require at least two men to capture. Push on and capture two more single capture zones to win.
  
Wehrmacht:
The "Station de Train" must be retaken and held by two men. Attack two more single capture zones to win.
