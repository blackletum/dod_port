Mission Brief
January, 1944, Italy. The Allies have prepared a simple, fast beach assault to capture the city of Anzio. Little did they know that the Germans were already there, waiting.

U.S. Army:
Five points to capture, split up and capture the Church Plaza and the Bridge, both are two man capture points, hold these while the rest of your team capture the single flags.
  
Wehrmacht:
Hold back the attack by capturing the Church Plaza and the Bridge first, both are two man capture points, hold these while the rest of your team capture the single flags and push the allied attack back to the sea.
