Mission Brief
Allied troops have entrenched defenses setup throughout the town of Jagd. If these defenses fall, the Axis will control another critical point in the push forward.

U.S. Army:
Defend these targets at all costs! If Axis troops get through and plant explosives, you may have time to disarm them.
  
Wehrmacht:
Pick up an explosives pack on your way through the church and plant it on one of the three objectives. Each objective will need two charges to destroy it.
