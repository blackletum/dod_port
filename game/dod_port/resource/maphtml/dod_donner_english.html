Mission Brief June, 1944, Normandy
The Allies struggle through the hedgerow country towards the key town of Saint Lo - the intersection for roads to key cities such as Caen and Carentan.

U.S. Army:
Five Capture zones, two men to capture the tank. two more points near the Wehrmacht start point. Watch for MG and sniper nests.
  
Wehrmacht:
Retake the Tank in the centre, defend your capture zones and push them back. Take the last two allied points to claim the town.
